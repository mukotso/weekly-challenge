About Society for Conversion GIS Membership

If you are a GIS professional or intrested in using GIS for conservation, you can become a memeber of SCGIS! The diverse membership includes members from many different countries in the world. Join this expanding network today!

Benefits

As an SCGIS member, you get access to a large and expanding network of conservation GIS professionals worldwide.And, you can meet many of your fellow members at the SCGIS annual conference in California! When you become a member , you can:

  • Be part of a dynamic community of a passionate GIS conservations that motivate and support each other Engage in discussions, seek advice, and get GIS technical support from other members on our website and listserv.
  • Access years of knowledge from past listserv email conservations via our listserv archive
    Get discounted registration for the SCGIS Annual Conference.
  • Attend free webinars offered to share technical and conservation knowledge with SCGIS members around the world.
  • Create a member profile, browse other member profiles, and send private messages to members
  • Post description of the GIS project you are working on
  • Create or join an SCGIS chapter
  • Browse and post GIS-related jobs on jobs board Post GIS-related news and events
